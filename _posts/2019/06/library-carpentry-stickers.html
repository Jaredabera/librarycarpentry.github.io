By Chris Erdmann

Stickers are a great way to share your support of Library Carpentry. We often get requests for stickers for this reason but sometimes we do not have enough available to provide them at every event. Thanks to the LIR HEAnet User Group for Libraries we will have plenty of stickers on hand for the attendees of our LIBER-LIR-Library Carpentry instructor training 2-day event.

They were able to print the stickers from the images in our Library Carpentry Style Guide created by Drew Heles and David Kane:

https://github.com/LibraryCarpentry/lc-styleguide

You too can print the stickers out using a local/online service, but if you would like, we can also send you stickers. For this reason, we have created this request form:

https://forms.gle/cDo6o9tQ7s88FEWX7

Please let us know by 30 June 2019 if you would like Library Carpentry stickers sent to you!
